Answer

This results in an `Uncaught SyntaxError: Invalid or unexpected token`, since thre are blank spaces after the backslash character on the 5th line of the paragraph.

Discussion

First off, there is an error in the program. There is a syntax error in assigning the paragraph to the paragraph variable. It may not be readily visible but there are whitespaces at the end of line 5 after the backward slash. The purpose of the backward slash is to escape the newline character at the end. However, with the extra whitespace this does not happen and thus leads to syntax error.

The program still has a bug after removing the extra whitespace at line 5. There are extra whitespaces in between some of the sentences. This is because it considers the whole string to be continuous. It counts the spaces at the beginning of each new line (line 2 to line 6) as part of the string.
